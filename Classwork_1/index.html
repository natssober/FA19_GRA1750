The Labrador Retriever, or just Labrator, is a large breed of retriever-gun dog.

The majority of the characterstics of the breed, with the exception of colour, are the results of breeding to produce a working retriever.

The Labrador is one of the most popular breeds of dog in Canada, the United Kingdom, and the United States.

Puppies of all colours can potially occur in the same litter.
